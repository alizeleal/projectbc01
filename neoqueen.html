Neo Queen Serenity

Over 1000 years old

Queen of Crystal Tokyo as well as all of Earth in the 30th Century.

Formerly known as Usagi Tsukino, she was the first to wake-up after a prolonged ice age that covered the Earth. With the power of the Silver Crystal, she got rid of the evil and sickness of the world, bringing peace and happiness.
