Queen Serenity

Age Unknown

Queen Serenity was the ruler of the Moon Kingdom during the time of the Silver Millennium, and is the mother of Princess Serenity.

Long ago, Queen Serenity ruled over the people of the Moon, who had long lives, given to aid their mission to protect the Silver Crystal, while at the same time watching over Earth and guiding it on a good path.
